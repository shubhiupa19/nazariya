# Nazariya: Product Description

## One-Sentence Summary
Nazariya is a real-time perspective correction tool that helps you think more clearly the moment comparison narrows your judgment.

## The Problem
Comparison spirals happen in seconds. You see someone's milestone and your brain instantly builds a story: *they're ahead, I'm behind, I'm not enough.* The visible event becomes a complete truth about your pace or worth. The pain is real. The logic is distorted.

## The Solution
Nazariya walks you through a three-minute structured reframe that separates what was visible from what is true. You input the moment and its automatic conclusion. Nazariya returns a clear breakdown of what actually happened, what context is missing, a sober reinterpretation, and a return to present reality. You choose a tone that fits: Gentle, Direct, or Analytical.

## Why This Matters
Comparison distorts decisions. It creates false urgency, erodes confidence, and makes you make choices you're not actually ready for. Corrected perspective isn't about feeling better—it's about thinking better.

## Key Differentiators

**No motivation or performance speech.** You don't leave inspired; you leave thinking clearly.

**No comparison-down.** It doesn't diminish the other person or ask you to feel better by finding someone worse off.

**No therapy substitute.** It's not diagnosis or trauma processing—just a calm intervention for a specific moment.

**Structurally honest.** The output is always four sections with specific purposes, written like a thoughtful friend, never like a chatbot or life coach.

## How It Works
1. You describe what you saw (the visible event) and what conclusion your brain attached to it.
2. You input what's actually true about your situation and constraints.
3. Optionally, you reference a built-in story about someone building their life under real constraints.
4. You choose your tone: Gentle (tender moments), Direct (cutting through noise), or Analytical (getting details clear).
5. Nazariya returns a structured reframe that restores proportion and clarity in under a minute.
6. Your reframe saves locally—nothing is sent to the server except the reframe request itself.

## What You Get
- **Immediate clarity**: See the distinction between what was visible, what was inferred, and what is actually true.
- **Sober perspective**: A reinterpretation that doesn't minimize your feeling but does correct the distortion.
- **Local storage**: Your entire history stays on your device. No profiles, no data collection, no algorithm.
- **Real examples**: Built-in stories about people building lives under real constraints (working night shifts while studying, learning to drive at thirty-four, etc.) so you're not alone in complexity.
- **Calm design**: An interface that's here when you need it, not demanding attention or performing urgency.

## Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15 with TypeScript, React 19, Tailwind CSS
- **AI**: OpenAI API with strict prompt engineering and JSON schema validation
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage for local history
- **Design**: Accessible, calm, focused on readability and clarity

## The Philosophy
Nazariya is deliberately modest. It's not trying to fix comparison spirals permanently or heal you. It's offering a clear moment when you're spiraling right now. It's a tool that respects your intelligence, your feeling, and your need to think straight.

## For Judges & Evaluators

**What this teaches:**
- How to handle distorted thinking with structure instead of platitudes
- How to design for specific moments instead of vague aspirations
- How to use AI for clarity instead of scale
- How to respect user agency and local data instead of building dependence

**What makes it credible:**
- The reframe structure is backed by cognitive distortion work but executed with respect for real-world complexity
- The no-BS philosophy means the output actually sounds like something a thoughtful person would say
- The stories included are humble and specific, not inspirational or generic
- The technical choices (localStorage, structured output, prompt guardrails) reflect real care about safety and privacy

**What it demonstrates:**
- Careful product thinking about a real (if quiet) problem
- Design discipline: each element serves the core purpose, nothing is extra
- Respect for user intelligence: no dark patterns, no unnecessary urgency, no false optimism
- Technical competence: proper error handling, structured output validation, prompt engineering for consistency
